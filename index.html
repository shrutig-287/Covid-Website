The pandemic has taken a toll on the lives of people since its very beginning and protecting our loved ones is becoming a crucial matter of concern. These hard times demand more awareness about the spread of the deadly corona virus and effective measures to beat it.

Prevention Methods

Wear A Face Mask

Ensure that your mouth and nose are properly covered by a face mask before stepping out of your place, to reduce the risk of infection.

Learn More

Wash Your Hands

Regularly wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or use a hand sanitizer to kill the germs and prevent spread.

Learn More

Avoid Close Contact

Stay at least 6 feet away from others and practice social distancing as much as possible as asymptomatic patients can also act as carriers of the virus.

Learn More

Lifestyle

Adopting a healthy life-style can boost your immunity to a great extent. Building your immunity will ultimately result in greater protection.

Learn More

Symptoms

One may experience some of the following symptoms on coming in contact with Covid-19 virus :

  • Fever
  • Tiredness
  • Dry Cough
  • Sore Throat
  • Aches and Pains
  • Shortness of Breath
Know more

Things to avoid -

The following things must be avoided as a precautionary measure:

  • Do Not Share Eating
  • Do Not Touch Your Face or Nose
  • Do Not Contact Sick People

Things to do -

There are some do's that need to be followed as well:

  • Wash Your Hands For 20 sec
  • Wear a Mask if Available
  • Seek Medical Care Regularly
